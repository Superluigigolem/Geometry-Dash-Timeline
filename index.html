Geometry Dash Timeline

August 13th 2013

Geometry Dash made by RobTopGames

Version 1.0 was the first Version of Geometry Dash. This Version has the least amount of popularity out of them all and only a few people can claim that they have played the game around this time. It is still referred to the "stepping stone for the later updates" by many Players. When the game released, it had 7 main levels. Stereo Madness, Back On Track, Polargeist, Dry Out, Base After Base, Cant Let Go and Jumper. In 1.0, you can only play as a cube or ship. There is also upside-down gameplay in 1.0.

10th September 2013

The first update comes out

Geometry Dash 1.1 introduces a new level called Time Machine. In 1.1 levels, you can go left instead of always going right. You also get to comment and react to Geometry Dash levels. There was one new icon and one new color added. And of course, bugfixes.

October 21st 2013

1.2 is out

Update 1.2 is the second major update of Geometry Dash. The level "Cycles" was made in 1.2, and there is a new gamemode called the Ball(Yes, BALLS). There are more rewards and two new icons available. Also more bugfixes...

November 20th 2013

Say hello to 1.3

Update 1.3 introduced Xstep and a leaderboard. They also added the Star System. There are more level effects and details. 10 new icons were added, and more achievements. But in this update, there was a new difficulty called "Demon" added. Bugfixes, as always.

December 26th 2013

Welcome 1.4!

Update 1.4 is the fourth major update of Geometry Dash. There is a new level called "Clutterfunk", and there are now size portals in Geometry Dash(you can be small). There are new unlockable ships, more level detail and effects, and secondary color for ships. Surely the bugs have gone away!

January 30th 2014

1.5 has released!

Update 1.5 is the 5th major update of Geometry Dash. Please welcome Theory of Everything to the game! Theory of Everything has a new gamemode, a UFO. The magenta jump pad and jump rings are now added, however they don't jump much. There are more level effects, details and 4 cubes, 3 ships and a color. Copy and Paste was added to the level editor. This update also let user levels be rated Demon. This update is slightly bigger and has more improvements to the game.

March 25th 2014

1.6 was born

Update 1.6 was the sixth major update to Geometry Dash released on 25 March 2014. It introduced 13th and 14th levels, Electroman Adventures featuring destructible blocks, and the first Demon level Clubstep featuring deceptive obstacles and a greater challenge. RobTop 4 new cubes, 2 new ships, 4 new balls, 4 new UFOs and 2 new colours, including the first epic color, shadow black. It also introduced Map Packs and secret coins, with all prior levels being updated to include 3 within each.

May 21st 2014

1.7 to the world of Geometry Dash

Update 1.7 was the seventh major update in Geometry Dash. It was released on 21 May 2014. It introduced Electrodynamix. Now there is an arrow that makes you slow down, or go faster(0.5x, 1x, 2x and 3x). It also adds a new colour mechanic, new backgrounds, a new auto difficulty, a rainbow trail, a glow feature, four new cubes, four new ships, three new balls, and three new UFOs.

Auguat 7th 2014

1.8 is a W8

Update 1.8 was the eighth update to Geometry Dash. It was released on 7th August 2014. It introduced Hexagon Force, with dual mode and slopes. It also introduces new editor features, tappable mainscreen icons, a new colour, four new cubes, two new ships, one new ball, and one new UFO.

November 19th 2014

Update 9

August 26th 2015

DOUBLE GEOMETRY DASH!!!!!

Update 2.0 was the tenth major update to Geometry Dash. It was released on 26 August 2015. This update introduced two new levels, Geometrical Dominator and Deadlocked with robot form with 6 designs. user coins, a secret vault, lots of new editor features, friending, following and messaging on user account systems, 4 new colours, 16 new cubes, 7 new ships, 8 new balls, 8 new UFOs, and two new trails.

January 16th 2017

The second part begins!

Update 2.1 is the eleventh official update to Geometry Dash. It was released on 16 January 2017 for Steam, and on 18 January for iOS, Android and Amazon. This update introduced one new main level, Fingerdash, the spider form, new jump rings, dash rings, custom gameplay rings, red jump pad, a red 4x speed portal, 2 new shops, 2 new vaults (Vault of Secrets and Chamber of Time), daily rewards, shards (fire, ice, poison, shadow and lava), lots of new editor features, mana orbs, diamonds, vault keys, five demon difficulties, the epic ranking, Gauntlets, Hall of Fame, quests, daily levels, death effects, more commenting features, options, optimizations, many new icons, colours, and many more features and secrets.

December 19th 2023

THE LONG WAIT...FINALLY

Update 2.2 is the twelfth major update to Geometry Dash, released on December 19th 2023 for iOS, Android, and Steam. [Twitter 1][YouTube 1] The update introduces the main level Dash, the new Swing gamemode, new triggers such as camera controls, post-processing effects for levels, triggers for movement, and more. Additionally, Update 2.2 added platformer levels that allow for free movement, alongside four levels(The Tower, The Sewers, The Cellar and The Secret Hallow) with the type in The Tower. It also added two new shops (the Mechanic and the Diamond Shop), numerous new icons, a second tier of shards, Lists, new icon kit customisation options such as ship trails and animations for certain gamemodes, and the Paths.
